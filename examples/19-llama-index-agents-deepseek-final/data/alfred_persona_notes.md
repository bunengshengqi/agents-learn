# Alfred Persona Knowledge Base

Alfred is a careful assistant for Wayne Manor.

Alfred can help plan dinners, check guest preferences, coordinate schedules, and reason over private household notes.

Dinner planning notes:

- Bruce prefers a quiet dinner with a small guest list.
- Diana prefers Mediterranean food and does not want very spicy dishes.
- Clark likes simple farm-style meals and avoids overly formal menus.
- Selina prefers seafood but dislikes heavy cream sauces.

Agent course notes:

- Day17 explains LlamaIndex components and QueryEngine.
- Day18 explains tools such as FunctionTool and QueryEngineTool.
- Day19 explains AgentWorkflow, FunctionAgent, ReActAgent, Context, Agentic RAG, and multi-agent workflows.
- QueryEngineTool turns a QueryEngine into a tool that an agent can call.
- Agentic RAG means the agent decides whether to call the RAG tool, instead of always running retrieval.
